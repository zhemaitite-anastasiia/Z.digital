What is SEO

Search Engine Optimisation or SEO - is the simple activity of ensuring a website can be found in search engines for words and phrases relevant to what the site is offering. In many respects, it's simply quality control for websites. Having said that, if there was ever an industry that was little understood by outsiders - it's SEO. But we got you.

Meta Tags and Title Descriptions

Meta Tags and Title Description - are important elements of your website’s content. This helps Search Engines understand what the page is about and index your web pages accordingly for relevant keywords or keyword phrases.

ALT-tags for images 

Also called "alt tags" and "alt descriptions," alt text is the written copy that appears in place of an image on a webpage if the image fails to load on a user's screen. This text helps screen-reading tools describe images to visually impaired readers and allows search engines to better crawl and rank your website.

Seo headers

h1, h2, h3

Your header tags provide structure and context for your website. Each header should give the reader an idea of the information they can glean from the paragraph text that follows below.

Website sitemap

XML sitemaps make it easier for search engine to read the content on your site and index the pages accordingly. As a result, this increases your chances of boosting the SEO ranking of your website.

File robots.txt

Robots.txt — is a text file, which controls how search engine spiders see and interact with your webpages.

SSL-certificate

HTTPS

SSL certificate keeps your info secure and protects your website and customer's info from hackers.

Favicon

Favicon — is a small 16×16 pixel icon that serves as branding for your website. Its main purpose is to help visitors locate your page easier when they have multiple tabs open.

404 Error Page

“PAGE NOT FOUND” — is probably the most annoying thing to look at, on a screen. This is a page nobody wants to land into. This page should show that particular page doesn't exist.

 

301 redirect

A 301 redirect is a permanent redirect from one URL to another. 301 redirects send site visitors and search engines to a different URL than the one they originally typed into their browser or selected from a search engine results page.

Domain address

Domain Nameis a website name that users type into the browser’s URL address bar to load a site. the right domain name allow to rank higher.
